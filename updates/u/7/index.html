2018 Columbus Qualifier

Submitted By Keely Testa (@asher_keely) on 2018-04-11 at 01:24
The Columbus Qualifier was our second event of the 2018 season. After sixty-six qualification matches, we were ranked sixth. At alliance selections we were the first pick of 3635, Flying Legion and rounded out our alliance with 6023, DISCBOTS. Sadly, 6023's robot took damage so we used our backup bot 3344, Robocopz. Our alliance competed extremely well in the playoffs, winning both our quaterfinals and semifinals matches undefeated. Our journey ended as finalists against the number one seed alliance which was composed of 4910, East Cobb Robotics, 2974, Walton Robotics, and 6925, W. A. Robotics. We received medals and a plaque for being finalists and were also awarded the Innovation in Controls Award.
