Destination Einstein 2018

Submitted By Keely Testa (@asher_keely) on 2018-02-18 at 22:45
We attended Destination Einstein in Colombus for the fourth year in a row. Our host, Columbus Space Program, was gracious enough to let us use their field as well as lend us several tools and parts we needed for our robot. We added roller intake wheels to our arms which has increased the efficiency of our intake. We discovered that with this new system we can launch cubes unto the switch as well as into the exchange. This experience has been beyond helpful and we are thankful for the invite.
