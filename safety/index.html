Safety Team

About

Throughout the season, safety team is responsible for keeping everyone safe. The members of this team ensure that no one gets injured and that nothing is left where people could get hurt. This includes making sure everyone is wearing safety gear and doing a quick check after each meeting. This teamâ€™s primary focus is prioritizing safety.

During Build-Season

Safety team members inspect other team members to make sure everyone is following the safety procedures. They must also make sure everyone clamps down and that no one is injured. As the robot is being built, these members will go around to make sure there is nothing left around that could bring harm to anyone.

During Competition

They make sure that everyone in the pit is following the safety rules and that the pit is safe and clean. These team members will administer help to small injuries if necessary and have a safety kit on them at all times in case of emergency. They are also responsible for talking to the judges and answering safety questions.
