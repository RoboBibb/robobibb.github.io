Community outreach

Community outreach allows us to spread interest in robotics and foster STEM in our community. Many people do not understand what robotics truly is. It is exciting to see their enthusiam after we explain to them what we are doing. It is rewarding to give back to the community that supports us. We care about Macon-Bibb!
